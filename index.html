About Us

Mwesigwa Associated Advocates is a full-service commercial law firm covering all areas of corporate and commercial practice, with expertise in banking and finance, insurance, commercial transactions, revenue law and taxation, litigation, alternative dispute resolution, land transactions and conveyances. It also has advocates with a wealth of knowledge in civil, criminal procedures, intellectual property law, labour laws and immigrations laws.

Our Vision
